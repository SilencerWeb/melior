Mila

Melior’s MILA chatbot, powered by advanced Artificial Intelligence to provide a better tool for product discovery and simpler navigation of large complex catalogues is an impressive e-commerce advantage for all businesses.

MILA can perform faster than its human equivalents; never makes the same mistake twice; is instantly scalable and costs a third of the price of a human agent. MILA is also capable of dealing with many more enquiries in the same 24 hour period.

MILA can instantly deal with queries that are beyond a human customer support operative. If a customer wants a specific product, for example, they need only ask MILA and all the product details appear within the chat (image, cost, size etc.). They can then purchase it easily from within the messaging application – the entire transaction carried out seamlessly as soon as the customer makes their decision to buy.

MILA is even able to help a customer when the name of a product escapes them. With a simple picture upload, our chatbot is able to deliver the product details within seconds.

Deployment is swift and pain free.

Subscribe instantly and add a chatbot to an e-commerce operation within a matter of days regardless of the industry. From fashion companies to building supplies; or chocolates to pizza delivery - MILA understands them all. MILA offers a saving of at least 60% against the fees that companies pay typically human customer services operators and the ability to scale up instantly at a pro-rata cost

Max

MAX - our interactive chatbot that never sleeps.

MAX, our FAQ bot, is equipped with easily extensible domain knowledge allowing it to helpfully address business-related Frequently Asked Questions like opening hours, methods of payment, shipping options and much more.

Speedy retrieval of key information is integral to aid customer engagement in their online experience, and to better assist them with their prospective purchases all around the clock.

MAX relies on a number of independent AI models to help it assess the intent of the utterance heard, the recipient (in case of group chat), and the sentiment and tone of the message. It is an effective tool for often repeated queries, to help with channel management and community support as well as with 1-to-1 question answering.

Minnie

MINNIE is our AI powered chatbot customised to small businesses.

As small business have different acute needs, we have created a solution allowing them to select key features according to their individual requirements.

We’ve combined MILA’s expertise with MAX’s superior technology at an affordable price for small and medium businesses with commensurate channels and rates.

Signing up to the MINNIE PAYG application does not cost a business any money – it is simply a refundable token-based deposit.
